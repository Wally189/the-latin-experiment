Reading · Augustine

Why write a confession addressed to God?

Augustine’s Confessions is not a transcript of private sacramental confession. It is prayer written for human readers: memory, sin, praise and conversion interpreted in the presence of God.

To develop: the meanings of confessio, the public purpose of the work and the difference between autobiography and theological prayer.

Worship · First impressions

Why the Mass can feel closed before its grammar is understood

Shared responses, gestures and silence can look like exclusion to a newcomer. Repetition may later reveal a stable form that does not depend upon the personality of the person leading it.

To develop: a plain guide to the shape of the Mass alongside an honest account of what first attendance can feel like.
