The field-guide method

Six signs, repeated.

Every developed page uses the same rhythm. It makes the source of a claim visible and gives the reader something concrete to do with it.

  1. LookBegin with what is actually there.
  2. AskFind the question beneath the argument.
  3. UnderstandExplain it in ordinary language.
  4. CheckGo to the Church’s own sources.
  5. WeighKeep objections and failures visible.
  6. TryRead, compare, observe or practise.
