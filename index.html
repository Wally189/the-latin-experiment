Schedule

Choose how many days each week you can study consistently. The aim is not to rush through a lesson, but to give it enough attention to read, write, listen, speak and return to anything that did not settle the first time.

30–45

Minutes a day

One focused study period is the target. A difficult lesson may need another day; the calculated finish date is a guide rather than a deadline.

Build your plan

Select up to seven days. The plan assumes one 30–45 minute session on each chosen day.

How Father Most structures the course: Volume I is not arranged around modern topics such as home, work or school. It is a graded reading course. New forms are introduced through increasingly demanding Latin passages, explained in familiar language and revisited in regular recapitulation lessons. The six blocks below are a reader-friendly map created for this website from the book’s published progression; they are not headings Father Most used himself.
