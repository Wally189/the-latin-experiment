A beginner's learning journey

Learning Latin, one lesson at a time.

I have wanted to understand more of the language that runs through Catholic prayer, music, history and thought. I found Father William Most's Latin by the Natural Method and decided to work through it from the beginning.

This site records what I learn, where I get stuck and what helps.

Why Latin?

Because it keeps appearing.

I meet Latin in the Mass, prayers, sacred music, inscriptions and Church documents. I do not want it to remain a collection of impressive-looking phrases. I want to understand what I am reading and hearing.

The aim is simple: follow a complete course, record the experience honestly, and make the difficult parts clearer for the next beginner.
