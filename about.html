About

Why I am learning Latin.

My name is Alan Gallagher. I am a complete beginner with a longstanding interest in the Catholic Church, its history and its language.

Latin keeps appearing in places I care about: prayer, music, liturgy, theology, law, inscriptions and the history of the Church. I have known isolated phrases for years, but I want to move beyond recognition and begin to understand the language itself.

I chose Father William Most's Latin by the Natural Method because it begins with connected Latin and lets the grammar emerge from what the learner is reading. I am working through the course from the beginning and recording the experience here.

The notes are personal, but the explanations are intended to be useful. When I misunderstand something, I will correct it rather than quietly rewrite the journey as though I understood everything at once.

The aim

Make the subject inviting without making it shallow.

I want the site to feel modern, colourful and interesting while treating the language and its sources seriously. It should be possible to arrive knowing nothing and still feel that Latin is within reach.
