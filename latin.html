Notebook · Latin

The Sign of the Cross, word by word.

The notebook strand can be practical as well as reflective. Here the site starts to show how learning the language of prayer can work page by page.

Look

Understand

A single prayer can become a small language lesson, a doctrinal lesson and a devotional practice at once.

This section will gradually grow into fuller guided Latin material. For now it shows the principle: look at the whole, then break it down without losing the prayer itself.

Check

Official source first

Church teaching
Catechism §2157The Sign of the Cross at the beginning of Christian prayer.
Try

Use it like a workbook

  • Read the whole prayer aloud once in English, once in Latin.
  • Notice that Patris, Filii and Sancti all show the same “of ...” structure.
  • Then make the Sign of the Cross prayerfully rather than treating the language note as an end in itself.
